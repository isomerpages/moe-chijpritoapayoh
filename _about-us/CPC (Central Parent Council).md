<h3>Join us as a Parent Volunteer!</h3>
<h4>YOU CAN PLAY A PART</h4>
<p>Each of us, as a parent, has a shared interest and responsibilities to
    our
    <br>children. The objective of the Central Parent Council (CPC) is to create
    these
    <br>partnerships between IJ parents, the school, to help all children in CHIJ
    <br>Primary to succeed in school and in life.</p>
<p>Walk with us, and join in the IJ family of Parent Volunteers (PVs), who
    work
    <br>together, pooling our individual skills and talents, motivated by a common
    <br>love, not just for our daughters, but for every child in our school.</p>
<p>“Do not walk in front of me,</p>
<p>I may not follow.</p>
<p>Don’t walk behind me,</p>
<p>I may not lead.</p>
<p>Walk beside me</p>
<p>And just be my friend.”</p>
<p>(Albert Camus)</p>
