<p>&nbsp;<strong><u>Overview</u></strong>
</p>
<p>The Handbells Ensemble was formed in 2003 to bring the music arts scene
in CHIJ Primary (Toa Payoh) to new heights. Currently, it consists of students
from Primary 3 to Primary 6, with a total of 44 students. The Handbells
Ensemble is a CCA that aims to expose the handbell ringers to a wide range
of different forms of music genre to further develop the potential in the
students.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>With maximum exposure and intensive training provided, the Handbells Ensemble
also aims to prepare the students to represent the school in various music
competitions and performances, not only locally but in the international
scene if the opportunity arises.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong><u>Learning Outcomes</u></strong>
</p>
<p>By participating in the Handbells Ensemble CCA, students will be able
to:</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Demonstrate good handbell
techniques.</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Master the techniques
and skills in playing handbells and handchimes.</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Learn music pieces to
perform for the school and the community.</p>
<p></p>
<p><strong><u>Achievements</u></strong>
</p>
<p>Singapore Youth Festival 2024 Arts Presentation – Certificate of Accomplishment</p>
