<p><strong><u>Overview</u></strong>
</p>
<p>Our Modern Dance CCA provides students with exposure to various dance
genres, techniques and moves. We strive to ignite the passion for dance
and appreciation for the Art form in each child. Our young dancers develop
poise, grace, musicality, agility and flexibility through our training
sessions. The opportunities for performing during school and nation-wide
events also instill self-discipline and promote confidence. With much hard
work and dedication, the Modern Dance members, guided by our talented instructors,
have taken the dance arts scene in CHIJ Primary (Toa Payoh) to greater
heights.</p>
<p></p>
<p><strong><u>Learning Outcomes</u></strong>
</p>
<p>By participating in the Modern Dance CCA, students will be able to:</p>
<p>●&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Gain exposure to varied dance forms, techniques
&amp; choreography. They will experience at least three different dance
genres or styles.</p>
<p>●&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Develop confidence and a sense of pride through
performing.</p>
<p>●&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Foster team building through group choreographies.</p>
<p></p>
<p><strong><u>Achievements</u></strong>
</p>
<p>Singapore Youth Festival 2024 Arts Presentation – Certificate of Distinction</p>
