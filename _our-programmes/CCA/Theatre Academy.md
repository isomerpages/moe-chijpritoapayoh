<p>&nbsp;<strong><u>Overview</u></strong>
</p>
<p>The Theatre Academy is a recreational drama CCA which provides a safe
space for students to pick up theatrical skills and experiment with character
role play.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>The CCA aims to provide opportunities for students to experience acting,
directing, script-writing and public speaking through various CCA activities.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong><u>Learning Outcomes</u></strong>
</p>
<p>By participating in the Theatre Academy CCA, students will be able to:</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Pick up basic acting
and public speaking skills.</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Develop communication
skills and teamwork.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong><u>Achievements</u></strong>
</p>
<p>Annual CHIJ Primary (Toa Payoh) Performing Arts Concert</p>
