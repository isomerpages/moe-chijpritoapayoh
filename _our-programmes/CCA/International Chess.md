<p><strong><u>Overview</u></strong>
</p>
<p>The International Chess Club CCA offers students the opportunity to learn
and improve their chess playing skills. It aims to nurture students’ interest
in chess and help them develop a range of skills such as critical thinking,
visualisation and strategic planning, while fostering character traits
such as sportsmanship and resilience.</p>
<p><strong><u>Learning Outcomes</u></strong>
</p>
<p>By participating in the International Chess Club CCA, students will be
able to:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Know basic rules and strategies of chess.&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Think critically, analyze situations, anticipate consequences and form
long-term plans.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Demonstrate sportsmanship in accepting losses, being gracious in victory
and maintaining respectful behaviour towards opponents.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;<strong><u>Achievements</u></strong>
</p>
<p>65th National Inter-School Team Chess Championships 2024</p>
<p>U12 Girls South Zone – 1st place</p>
<p>U10 Girls South Zone – 1st and 2nd place</p>
<p>U8 Girls South Zone – 1st and 3rd place</p>
