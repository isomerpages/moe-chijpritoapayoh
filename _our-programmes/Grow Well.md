<p>The Grow Well @ CHIJ Primary initiative aims to support the nation’s direction
in promoting national health through early intervention to shape healthy
habits in our students from young. We hope to develop a community of learners
who will take ownership in cultivating a lifelong healthy lifestyle.</p>
<p>We will be adopting the 4 ‘I’s approach (Integrate, Innovate, Intervene,
Involve) to provide us with clarity and to guide us to be more targeted
in our Grow Well @ CHIJ Primary strategies.</p>
<p><strong><u>Integrate</u></strong>
</p>
<p>Incorporate Grow Well messages and content in existing structures and
programme:</p>
<p>· PE Curriculum</p>
<p>· CCE (FTGP) Curriculum</p>
<p>· CCE (MTL) Curriculum</p>
<p>· Level Morning Moves / Recess Play</p>
<p><strong><u>Innovate</u></strong>
</p>
<p>Introduce new ideas and artefacts to complement existing school programmes:</p>
<p>· Grow Well Focus Week</p>
<p>· Grow Well Termly Challenge</p>
<p>· Health Plan Materials</p>
<p><strong><u>Intervene</u></strong>
</p>
<p>Leverage on data to help address current health trends and issues among
the students:</p>
<p>· School Health Data</p>
<p>· Termly Check-In Survey</p>
<p><strong><u>Involve</u></strong>
</p>
<p>Having parents on-board to support the Grow Well initiative through various
platforms:</p>
<p>· Grow Well Champion Challenge</p>
<p>· Communication with Parents</p>
<p>By driving the 4 ‘I’s approach through effective collaboration between
the different departments in the school and harnessing support from parents
and other external partners, we are confident that we will be able to help
our IJ girls Grow Well and Live Well.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
